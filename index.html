Cell Phone Unlocking

This job was requested by the owner of a private business to offer an online rental service for cell phone decoder boxes. The owner wanted a quick job with few implementations, as well as an interface predefined by him. The work was completed within the specified period of 7 days, but after that period he wanted to add other functionalities. Being a short-term project, there was a problem in the code when displaying the name of the logged in user, which was resolved with an iteration since the 3rd time the data was requested it returned the correct string.

The work was completed satisfactorily.


NodeJS, MongoDB, Express were used here.

Tournaments Page

This project was carried out for the Cuban Dota2 community, with the purpose of having a page in which to manage, display and provide information about the amateur tournaments that were going to be developed.

The page was developed in 2 weeks in which it was completed without problems and without delays.


NodeJS, MongoDB, Express were used here.
